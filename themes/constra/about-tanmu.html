Who We Are

Tanmu was founded in order to provide clients with projects with project management services up to the international standards, that are only available to the large scale projects due to the budget constraints. Through utilizing the high quality skillful professionals, modern techniques and effective procedures, our clients will be highly served in a very cost effective manner.

“Being a Project Manager is like being an artist, you have the different colored process streams combining into a work of art.” – Greg Cimmarrusti

We provide highly qualified and experienced professionals with global and local knowledge that will cover all aspects of Planning, Project Management, and Cost Management to successfully deliver the project at each stage. We deliver effective cost management services tailored to each of our Client's specific needs. Our Clients trust us to help them resolve their disputes and risks associated with the Construction.
